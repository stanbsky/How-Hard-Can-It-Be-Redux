Week 1

Got to know each other and discussed some basics of how we would approach the project. Brainstormed about the idea related to team identity, and structure of teamwork.

25th Nov 2021

Finalized decision on task assignment for present members.

We have just completed the initial forming of requirements (T4) based on

Implementation of the game discussed. The progress of the method

14th Dec 2021

The primary purpose of the meeting was to make sure there was a

28th Jan 2022

2nd Feb 2022
